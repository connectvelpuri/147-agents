## Creating a New Industry Profile

Each industry profile should follow this template:

1. **Industry Overview** — Why this vertical needs different CRM
2. **Custom Fields & Objects** — Extensions to the base data model
3. **Pipeline Stages** — Vertical-specific deal stages
4. **Workflows & Automations** — Key business processes
5. **Dashboard Layouts** — Role-specific views
6. **Reports** — Standard reporting pack
7. **Integration Points** — Common third-party tools
8. **Compliance Considerations** — Regulatory requirements
