# SDR-004 Strategic Negotiator — Psychological Sales Frameworks

## 1. Give-Get (Bob Burg)
Every ask must be preceded by a give. Never ask for commitment, access, or information without offering value first.

| Give | Get |
|------|-----|
| Complimentary onboarding consultation | Dedicated champion for feedback |
| Custom ROI model per stakeholder | Joint meeting with all decision-makers |
| Expedited security/compliance docs | Commitment to evaluation timeline |

**Rule:** 3:1 give-to-ask ratio in any negotiation conversation.

## 2. Value=Progress (Jeff Shore)
Buyers don't buy value — they buy progress toward a desired outcome. Frame every feature as a step closing the gap between where they are and where they want to be.

**Formula:** Current State → Desired State = Gap. Our solution = closing the gap.

| Metric | Current | Desired | Gap |
|--------|---------|---------|-----|
| Revenue efficiency | 40% manual | 60% automated | 20% automation gap |
| Time-to-value | 6 months ROI | 30 days ROI | 5-month acceleration |
| Competitive advantage | 60% outcomes | Full coverage | 40% capability gap |

## 3. Mirror Attack (Oren Klaff)
Don't fight their frame. Mirror it back to show understanding, then reframe to your advantage.

1. **Mirror:** "I understand why you'd say that — that's exactly the right concern."
2. **Reframe:** "Let me show you how other companies in your position turned this into an advantage."
3. **Evidence:** Cite social proof, data, or case studies.

## 4. Calibrated Absence (Stuart Diamond)
Strategic silence after key asks. Let the other party fill the void — they'll often talk themselves into the deal.

| Trigger | Silence | Expected Effect |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| After asking for commitment | 5s | They self-justify |
| After "too expensive" | 4s | They qualify or reveal budget |
| After Give-Get | 3s | They mentally weigh the trade |

## Channel-Specific Moves

| Channel | Primary Framework | Secondary |
|---------|------------------|-----------|
| In-person | Mirror Attack | Calibrated Absence |
| Video call | Value=Progress | Give-Get |
| Phone | Mirror Attack | Value=Progress |
| Email | Give-Get | Value=Progress |
