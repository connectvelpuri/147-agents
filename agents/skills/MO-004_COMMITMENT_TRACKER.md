# MO-004 Skill: Commitment Tracker

## Framework: Andy Paul — Dual WIIFM Commitment Tracking
Track commitments against both organizational and personal WIIFM:

### Organizational Commitments
- "We need to solve X by Q3" (timeline commitment)
- "Our target is to increase Y by Z%" (metric commitment)
- "We've allocated budget for this" (resource commitment)
- "I've scheduled a review with the team" (process commitment)
- "Here are the criteria we'll evaluate against" (evaluation commitment)

### Personal Commitments
- "This would make my team's life easier" (team benefit)
- "I'd look good if this works" (career benefit)
- "I'm tired of dealing with X" (pain motivation)
- "My boss wants to see progress here" (upward pressure)
- "This is my initiative" (ownership commitment)

### Red Flags (Verbal Commitments Without Action)
- "Sounds good, let me think about it" — no timeline, no next step
- "Send me info" — passive receipt, not active engagement
- "I'll circle back" — no date specified
- "Let me discuss with the team" — they're delegating, not owning
- "We're interested but not ready" — no defined ready criteria

## Commitment Escalation Funnel

| Level | Definition | Signal | Action |
|-------|------------|--------|--------|
| L0: None | No commitment | Ghosting, deflection | Break-up, new angle |
| L1: Passive | Verbal interest | "Sounds interesting" | Ask for specific interest |
| L2: Active | Takes action | Attends call, reads materials | Move to evaluation |
| L3: Investment | Invests time/resources | Discovery, demo, trial | Lock in next step |
| L4: Social | Tells others | Introduces stakeholders | Support internal sell |
| L5: Transactional | Commits to buy | Contract, PO | Route to close |

## Framework: Calibrated Absence (Andy Paul)
- After a commitment is made, create space for them to miss you
- Follow-up at 80% of expected timing, not 100%
- Silence after value = tension → they re-engage
- If you're always chasing, they never commit

## Tracking Template
```
Commitment: {description}
Level: {L0-L5}
Type: {organizational / personal}
Date Made: {date}
Date Kept: {date or null}
Source: {call / email / meeting}
Verification: {evidence}
Follow-up Needed: {yes/no — what / when}
```
