## 3. Critical Gaps — Workflows MISSING from Most CRMs

> These are workflows that standard CRMs (SFDC, HubSpot, Dynamics) do NOT handle natively and require third-party apps, custom code, or manual processes.

| Gap | Module | Impact | Typical Workaround |
|-----|--------|--------|--------------------|
| **Lead enrichment automation** | Sales | Data quality, rep time wasted | ZoomInfo/Clearbit add-on |
| **CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote)** | Sales | Errors in quotes, approval friction | Salesforce CPQ, DealHub, Zuora |
| **Multi-wave campaign orchestration** | Marketing | Low engagement from batch-and-blast | Marketo/SFMC automation |
| **ABM orchestration** | Marketing | Weak account-level targeting | Demandbase, 6sense |
| **Omnichannel conversation routing** | Service | Fragmented customer experience | Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk |
| **Self-service portal with deflection** | Service | High ticket volume, costly CS | Zendesk Guide, Salesforce Service Cloud |
| **SLA breach escalation** | Service | Missed deadlines, penalties | Custom flows, third-party |
| **CSAT/NPS closed-loop action tracking** | Service | Low follow-through on feedback | Manual spreadsheets |
| **Subscription billing & proration** | Finance | Billing errors, revenue leakage | Zuora, Chargebee, Recurly |
| **Revenue recognition (ASC 606)** | Finance | Compliance risk, manual journaling | Zuora RevPro, NetSuite |
| **Commission calculation** | Finance | Errors, disputes, low rep trust | Xactly, Spiff, CaptivateIQ |
| **Expense management** | Finance | Manual approval, policy enforcement | Expensify, Concur |
| **Forecast accuracy tracking** | Sales/Finance | Biased forecasts, no calibration | Custom dashboards |
| **Data quality scoring** | Ops | CRM decay over time | DemandTools, DataFox |
| **User access reviews** | Ops/Security | Compliance gaps, over-permissioned users | Manual CSV exports |
| **GDPR right-to-erasure workflow** | Ops | Manual multi-object deletion | Custom Apex/scripts |
| **Integration sync error handling** | Ops | Data sync failures, no retry | Custom middleware |
| **Lead recycling & nurture re-entry** | Sales/Marketing | Leads lost after rejection | Custom automation |
| **Account health scoring** | Service/Sales | Reactive vs proactive retention | Gainsight, Totango |
| **Sales cadence response detection** | Sales | Reps don't know when to follow up | Outreach, SalesLoft |

## 8. Pain Points Research Cross-Reference

### Top CRM Pain Points (Industry Research 2024-2026)
1. **Manual data entry** → Workflows: Lead Capture (insufficient auto-capture), Contact Creation, Custom Field Propagation
2. **Poor data quality** → Workflows: Dedup (2.2, 3.3), Data Quality Monitoring (Ops 3.4), Enrichment (1.2)
3. **Low user adoption** → Workflows: Discount Approval (D), MQL→SAL Handoff (D), Commission (D), Contact Merge (D)
4. **Slow lead response time** → Workflows: Lead Distribution (1.5), MQL→SAL Handoff (CW-1.1), SLA Management (SV-5.1)
5. **No 360-degree view** → Workflows: Cross-module Handoffs (all CW), Account Health (SW-3.5), Relationship Mapping (SW-3.3)
6. **Reporting fragmentation** → Workflows: Report→Dashboard (CW-7.1), Attribution (MW-3.3), Forecast (SW-8.1)
7. **Integration failures** → Workflows: Webhook (CW-6.1), Third-Party Connections (Ops 5.2), Sync Monitoring (Ops 5.3)
8. **Manual forecasting** → Workflows: Forecast Pipeline (SW-8.1), Pipeline-to-Revenue (FW-5.1), Probability (SW-4.3)
9. **SLA non-compliance** → Workflows: SLA Management (SV-5.1/5.2), Breach Escalation
10. **Billing errors** → Workflows: Quote-to-Order (SW-5.1), Invoice (FW-1.3), Subscription Mgmt (FW-2.1)

### Critical Path for Remediation (Priority Order)
```
1. Fix MQL→SAL Handoff (highest friction, biggest revenue impact)
2. Automate Lead Distribution + SLA (speed to lead)
3. Improve Data Quality (dedup + enrichment + scoring)
4. Streamline Discount Approval (velocity killer)
5. Automate Commission (rep trust and adoption)
6. Integrate Service→Sales handoff (churn prevention)
7. Fix Forecast Accuracy (executive confidence)
8. Implement Cross-Module Reporting (unified view)
```
