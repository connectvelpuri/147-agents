# Sovereign CRM — Sales One-Pager

## Tagline
The open-source CRM that respects your data, your budget, and your team.

## One Sentence
Self-hosted CRM with custom fields, pipeline Kanban, and lead scoring —
free for 5 users, $29/seat for teams, open-source under AGPL.

## What It Replaces
Salesforce Essentials ($25/seat), HubSpot Starter ($20/seat), Zoho ($14/seat)

## Key Features (What's Built)
- Multi-tenant RBAC with field-level permissions
- Contacts & Organizations with dedup
- Custom Fields (9 types — text, number, date, boolean, select, multi, URL, email, phone)
- Global Search across contacts, orgs, leads, and deals
- Lead Management with 7-factor auto-scoring
- Pipeline Kanban with drag-to-move and stage probabilities
- Deal Tracking with weighted forecasting
- CSV Import/Export
- Webhooks (8 event types, HMAC signed)
- Audit Logging & Compliance Matrix (GDPR/SOC2/HIPAA mapped)
- Setup Wizard (5-step onboarding)

## Who It's For
**ICP:** IT Consulting firms (15-200 people), SaaS startups (10-50 people), and
any team that has outgrown spreadsheets but doesn't want Salesforce complexity.

**Not for:** Enterprise sales teams that need SSO/SAML today, mobile-heavy field sales,
or teams that want turnkey cloud with no ops involvement.

## Business Model
- Open Source Core (AGPL v3) — free forever, self-hosted
- Commercial License — for teams that need proprietary modifications
- Cloud Hosting ($29/seat) — for teams that don't want to self-host
- Enterprise Support ($10K+/yr) — SLAs, dedicated support, compliance certs
