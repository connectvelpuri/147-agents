# Dogfood Plan — Run Sovereign CRM for Its Own Development

## Why
- Eat your own dog food. If we can't use it to track our own work, it's not ready.
- Every bug or missing feature we hit is one a customer would hit.
- It forces us to eat our own cooking on performance, UX, and reliability.

## What to Track Inside Sovereign CRM

### Organizations
- "Sovereign CRM" — the company/org

### Contacts
- All team members (developer + sales guy + future contributors)

### Pipeline Stages
1. Backlog (0%)
2. Sprint Planning (10%)
3. In Progress (30%)
4. In Review (50%)
5. Testing (70%)
6. Done (100%)
7. Cancelled (0%)

### Deals (Features)
Each feature sprint is a "Deal" moving through the pipeline:
- Sprint 4: Validate + Launch → in progress
- Sprint 5: Email + SSO → Backlog
- Sprint 6: Mobile PWA → Backlog

### Activities
- Log calls with prospects (type: call)
- Log architecture decisions (type: note)
- Log feature completions (type: task completion)

## Go Live Date
Start tracking Sprint 4 tasks inside Sovereign CRM immediately.
