## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document identifies critical gaps frequently overlooked in CRM implementation planning that would be flagged in a Big 4 strategic audit. While technical specifications and feature lists are well-documented, these governance, risk, and strategic elements are often missing from initial planning phases but become critical success factors during execution.

## 1. GOVERNANCE & COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK (Frequently Missing)

### 1.1 Data Governance Policies
- **Missing:** Formal data governance council structure and decision-making process
- **Missing:** Data stewardship roles and responsibilities definition
- **Missing:** Data quality standards and measurement procedures
- **Missing:** Master Data Management (MDM) governance model
- **Missing:** Data retention and archival policies aligned with regulatory requirements

### 1.2 Regulatory Compliance Mapping
- **Missing:** Comprehensive regulatory mapping (GDPR, CCPA, SOX, industry-specific)
- **Missing:** Data subject rights request procedures (access, deletion, portability)
- **Missing:** Privacy impact assessment (PIA) methodology
- **Missing:** Consent management framework documentation
- **Missing:** Cross-border data transfer compliance measures

### 1.3 Audit & Control Framework
- **Missing:** Audit trail design beyond basic logging (who, what, when, why, how)
- **Missing:** Segregation of duties (SoD) analysis for CRM functions
- **Missing:** Access control review procedures and frequency
- **Missing:** Exception reporting and investigation procedures
- **Missing:** Internal audit readiness checkpoints

### 1.4 Change Management Governance
- **Missing:** Configuration change approval process (CCAB)
- **Missing:** Emergency change procedures and controls
- **Missing:** Configuration baseline management
- **Missing:** Change impact assessment procedures
- **Missing:** Post-implementation review requirements
