**Phase 1: Accusation Audit**
"You're probably thinking we're expensive for a local firm. You might be wondering if we've done this before. And you're definitely concerned about the 12-month timeline."

**Phase 2: Mirror & Label**

- "How does a 6-month payback compare to Oracle's 18-month?"

Get agreement 3 times:
1. "Does 30% cost savings sound right?" -> Yes
2. "Achieving that in 6 months is valuable?" -> Yes
3. "So investing $1.8M to save $14.7M makes financial sense?" -> Yes

"CFO, if we don't solve maverick spend, what happens next year? And the year after?
What is the 5-year cost of NOT doing this?"
