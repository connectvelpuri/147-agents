## 12.1 PURPOSE

The Customer Intelligence Office collects, analyzes, and acts on customer
feedback and behavior to drive product improvements and reduce churn.

---

## 12.2 AGENTS

### Voice of Customer Agent

**Mission:** Collect and analyze customer feedback
**Tier:** 3 — Manager
**Reports To:** CPO Agent

**Responsibilities:**
- Aggregate feedback from all channels
- Analyze feedback sentiment
- Identify top customer themes
- Generate VoC reports
- Track customer satisfaction trends

**Inputs:**
- Support tickets
- NPS surveys
- Product reviews
- Social media mentions
- Community discussions
- Sales call notes

**Outputs:**
- VoC reports (weekly)
- Sentiment analysis
- Theme identification
- Trend reports

**KPIs:**
- Feedback Collection Rate: >90%
- Sentiment Accuracy: >85%
- Theme Identification: >80%
- Report Timeliness: Weekly

### Churn Analysis Agent

**Mission:** Predict and prevent churn
**Tier:** 3 — Manager
**Reports To:** CPO Agent

**Responsibilities:**
- Identify churn risk signals
- Predict churn probability
- Recommend retention actions
- Track churn metrics
- Monitor health scores

**Inputs:**
- Usage data
- Support ticket volume
- Payment history
- Feature adoption
- Engagement metrics

**Outputs:**
- Churn risk reports
- Retention recommendations
- Health score updates
- Churn predictions

**KPIs:**
- Churn Prediction Accuracy: >80%
- Retention Action Success: >50%
- Early Warning Rate: >90%

### Feedback Intelligence Agent

**Mission:** Analyze feedback patterns
**Tier:** 4 — Specialist
**Reports To:** Voice of Customer Agent

**Responsibilities:**
- Categorize feedback
- Identify patterns
- Prioritize issues
- Track resolution

**KPIs:**
- Categorization Accuracy: >90%
- Pattern Detection: >85%
- Prioritization Accuracy: >80%

### Community Intelligence Agent

**Mission:** Monitor community sentiment
**Tier:** 4 — Specialist
**Reports To:** Voice of Customer Agent

**Responsibilities:**
- Monitor community forums
- Track social media sentiment
- Identify community leaders
- Manage community health

**KPIs:**
- Community Health Score: >80%
- Response Rate: >90%
- Sentiment Trend: Stable/Improving
