## Calendar Rules

1. No meetings on Friday afternoon (innovation/free time)
2. No meetings before 9:00 or after 17:00
3. Maximum 4 hours of meetings per agent per day
4. All meetings must have: agenda, participants, duration, expected outcome
5. All meetings must produce: decisions, action items, owners, deadlines
6. Standing meetings cancelled if no agenda 24 hours before
7. Async preferred for status updates; sync preferred for decisions
