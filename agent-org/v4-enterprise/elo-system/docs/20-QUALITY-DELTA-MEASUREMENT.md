# ELO DOCUMENT 20: QUALITY DELTA MEASUREMENT SYSTEM
# Enterprise Learning Operations — How Output Quality Is Tracked

---

## Quality Delta Definition
Quality Delta = (Today's Output Quality Score) - (Yesterday's Output Quality Score)
Range: -10 (significant decline) to +10 (significant improvement)

---

## Quality Dimensions

| Dimension | Weight | Measurement Method |
|-----------|--------|-------------------|
| Technical Depth | 25% | Code review scores, architecture decision quality |
| Applied Practice | 25% | Feature completion quality, bug rate, production incidents |
| Strategic Thinking | 20% | Decision quality, planning accuracy, risk identification |
| Creativity | 15% | Novel solutions, innovation contributions, approach variety |
| Human Context | 15% | User empathy, customer understanding, stakeholder communication |

---

## Measurement Process

### Daily Measurement (At 19:00 Cycle)
1. Tier 3 practitioner analyst collects output samples
2. Output scored across 5 dimensions (1-10 each)
3. Daily quality score = weighted average
4. Quality Delta = today's score - yesterday's score
5. Delta logged in agent's quality history

### Weekly Aggregation
1. Daily deltas averaged for weekly quality delta
2. Trend analysis (improving, stable, declining)
3. Stagnation detection (3+ days same delta)
4. Domain-level quality delta calculated
5. Enterprise-level quality delta calculated

### Monthly Analysis
1. 30-day quality trend analyzed
2. Correlation with learning activity measured
3. Learning ROI estimated (quality improvement per learning item)
4. Certification impact measured (quality change after certification)
5. Cross-domain transfer impact measured

## Quality Score Ranges

| Range | Label | Interpretation |
|-------|-------|----------------|
| 9-10 | Exceptional | World-class output quality |
| 7-8 | Strong | Above expectations |
| 5-6 | Adequate | Meets expectations |
| 3-4 | Below | Needs improvement |
| 1-2 | Critical | Immediate intervention needed |

---

## Quality Delta Thresholds

| Delta | Label | Action |
|-------|-------|--------|
| +3 to +10 | Significant improvement | Celebrate, study what worked |
| +1 to +2 | Mild improvement | Note and reinforce |
| 0 | Stable | Monitor, look for opportunities |
| -1 to -2 | Mild decline | Investigate, minor intervention |
| -3 to -10 | Significant decline | Immediate investigation, intervention |

---

## Quality Correlation with Learning

The system tracks:
- Quality delta after learning pack consumption
- Quality delta after certification completion
- Quality delta after cross-domain transfer
- Quality delta after practitioner case study
- Quality delta after applied exercise

This enables:
- Which learning types produce the most quality improvement
- Which sources produce the highest quality delta
- Which domains benefit most from learning
- Which agents are most responsive to learning
