# DELIVERABLE 14: ENTERPRISE REVIEW BOARDS
# Sovereign Enterprise — Board Details

---

## Board Operating Principles

1. Every board has a clear charter, membership, and decision authority
2. Board decisions are binding — agents must comply unless they formally appeal
3. Board members rotate every 6 months to prevent groupthink
4. Board proceedings are recorded as decision records in Plane
5. Board exceptions require explicit justification and tracking

---

## Board 1: Executive Council

**Charter:** Set strategic direction, allocate investment, resolve executive conflicts
**Chair:** CEO (EXE-01)
**Members:** All 15 L1 executives
**Frequency:** Weekly (1 hour), Monthly (half-day strategic review)
**Quorum:** 8 of 15 members
**Voting:** Simple majority, chair breaks ties

**Standing Agenda (Weekly):**
1. Portfolio health dashboard (5 min)
2. Strategic initiative status (10 min)
3. Cross-functional escalations (15 min)
4. Market/competitive intelligence (10 min)
5. Decisions requiring executive alignment (15 min)
6. Action items from last meeting (5 min)

**Standing Agenda (Monthly):**
1. Full portfolio review (30 min)
2. Financial performance review (20 min)
3. Customer health review (20 min)
4. Technology health review (20 min)
5. People/organization review (20 min)
6. Strategic decisions requiring deeper discussion (60 min)
7. Next month priorities (30 min)

**Authority:**
- Approve/reject annual strategy
- Approve/reject major investments (>$50K)
- Approve/reject new product launches
- Approve/reject executive hiring/firing
- Resolve executive-level conflicts
- Set company-wide policies

## Board Meeting Recording

Every board meeting produces a Decision Record:

```json
{
  "board": "Board name",
  "date": "Meeting date",
  "attendees": ["List of attendees"],
  "quorum_met": true,
  "decisions": [
    {
      "decision": "Description of decision",
      "rationale": "Why this decision was made",
      "dissent": "Any dissenting views",
      "action_items": [
        {"owner": "Agent ID", "action": "What to do", "deadline": "When"}
      ]
    }
  ],
  "next_meeting": "Date and focus"
}
```
