# DELIVERABLE 5: CENTERS OF EXCELLENCE
# Sovereign Enterprise — 10 Centers of Excellence

---

## What Is a Center of Excellence (CoE)?

A CoE is a cross-functional group of experts who define standards, share knowledge,
and ensure consistency in their domain across ALL product lines.

CoEs are NOT reporting lines. They are knowledge-sharing and standards-governing bodies.
Agents REPORT to their product line but CONTRIBUTE to their CoE.

---

## The 10 Centers of Excellence

### CoE 1: Product CoE
**Lead:** CPO (L1)
**Members:** All Product Managers, Product Directors, Product Operations
**Scope:** Product strategy, value cases, roadmap, prioritization
**Standards:** PRD template, discovery process, feature scoring, roadmap format
**Cadence:** Weekly Product Council, Monthly Product Review
**Artifacts:** Product playbook, Discovery toolkit, Roadmap template, Feature scoring matrix

## CoE Operating Model

Each CoE follows this pattern:

1. **Standards Definition** — CoE defines standards for its domain
2. **Knowledge Sharing** — CoE shares best practices across product lines
3. **Review & Governance** — CoE reviews work in its domain for compliance
4. **Evolution** — CoE updates standards based on learnings

CoE authority is ADVISORY, not COMMAND. The CoE recommends standards.
Product lines ADOPT or EXCEPTION them. The Product Director has final say
on what their product line uses, subject to security and compliance requirements.
