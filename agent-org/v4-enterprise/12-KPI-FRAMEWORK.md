# DELIVERABLE 12: KPI FRAMEWORK
# Sovereign Enterprise — Metrics for Every Role

---

## KPI Principles

1. Every role has leading AND lagging indicators
2. Leading indicators predict future performance
3. Lagging indicators confirm past performance
4. KPIs are reviewed weekly at team level, monthly at product level, quarterly at executive level
5. KPIs that are not acted upon are removed — no vanity metrics
