# DELIVERABLE 2: ORGANIZATIONAL BLUEPRINT
# Sovereign Enterprise — Complete Organizational Structure

---

## DESIGN PRINCIPLES

1. Independence: Each product line operates autonomously
2. Shared Platform: Infrastructure is shared, products are not
3. Equal Partnership: Human and agents make decisions together
4. Governed Autonomy: Agents propose, human approves
5. Evaluated First: Every design choice validated before implementation
6. Scalable: Structure works for 1 product or 100
7. Observable: Every action tracked, every decision recorded
8. Evolving: Structure improves through retrospectives
