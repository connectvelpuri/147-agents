# Competitive Analysis: Copper CRM

**Phase 1 — Competitive Research | Created:** 2026-06-09
**Focus:** Google Workspace-Native CRM — Gmail Integration Depth
**Target:** Sovereign CRM Product Blueprint

---

## 1. COMPANY OVERVIEW

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Founded | 2013 (as ProsperWorks, rebranded Copper 2019) |
| Parent | Copper Inc. (Private) |
| Primary Customers | SMB, Google Workspace users |
| Pricing Model | Per-user/month (Basic/Professional/Business/Enterprise) |
| Deployment | Multi-tenant cloud only |
| Target Verticals | General SMB, Professional Services, Tech |

## 2. CORE MODULES

**CRM Core:** Contacts, Companies, Deals, Leads, Activities, Pipeline
**Gmail Integration:** Side panel in Gmail, auto-sync emails to records, click-to-create contacts from emails
**Google Calendar:** Event sync, meeting logging, activity tracking
**Google Drive:** Document attachment/linking from Drive
**Google Contacts:** Two-way sync with Google Contacts
**Workflow Automation:** Trigger-action rules, email templates, task automation
**Reporting:** Dashboards, deal progression, activity stats, custom reports
**Platform:** REST API, Google Apps Script integration, Zapier

## 3. STRENGTHS (Top 10)

1. **Deepest Gmail/Google integration** — side panel works seamlessly, email auto-logging
2. **Zero data entry from Gmail** — create contacts/companies from emails with one click
3. **Google Contacts sync** — two-way sync keeps contacts in sync with Google ecosystem
4. **Calendar activity logging** — automatically logs meetings to CRM records
5. **Simple, clean interface** — minimal learning curve for Google users
6. **Fast to adopt** — if your org is on Google Workspace, Copper is 30-min setup
7. **Good for professional services** — intuitive for small teams of consultants
8. **Workflow builder** — decent trigger-action automation for SMB needs
9. **Mobile app** — solid mobile experience, Google-integrated
10. **Apps Script extensibility** — Google Apps Script can extend Copper

## 4. WEAKNESSES — OUR OPPORTUNITIES (Top 12)

1. **Expensive for a simple CRM** — Professional at $23/user/month, Business at $55/user. Limited features for the price
2. **No marketing automation** — no campaigns, email sequences beyond basics, no landing pages
3. **No customer service** — no ticketing, knowledge base, SLA management
4. **No built-in calling** — no VoIP, no call recording, no power dialer
5. **Limited deal management** — no forecasting, no product catalog, no CPQ
6. **Account management basic** — no hierarchy, no relationship maps
7. **Limited customization** — custom fields only, no custom objects, rigid data model
8. **No AI features** — no lead scoring, no predictive AI, no conversation intelligence
9. **No offline** — full cloud dependency
10. **No enterprise features** — no sandboxes, limited RBAC, minimal audit
11. **Google-only** — no Outlook integration. Microsoft shops can't use it
12. **Reporting is basic** — dashboards are simple, no BI / embedded analytics

## 6. OUR OPPORTUNITIES AGAINST COPPER

1. **Unified platform** — Copper is sales-only. We include marketing + service
2. **AI-native** — Copper has no AI. We ship MCP-native AI
3. **Multi-platform** — not Google-only. Works with any email provider
4. **Offline-first CRDT** — Copper has no offline
5. **Enterprise depth** — Copper is SMB-only
6. **Custom objects** — Copper is rigid
7. **Self-hosted privacy** — Copper is cloud-only
8. **Better value** — Copper is expensive for basic features
