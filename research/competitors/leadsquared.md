## 2. CORE MODULES

**Sales CRM:** Leads, Contacts, Accounts, Deals, Pipeline, Activities
**Lead Management:** Lead capture, lead scoring, lead assignment, lead routing
**Sales Automation:** Workflows, Sequences (whatsapp, email, SMS)
**Mobile Sales:** Field sales app with GPS, visit logging
**Marketing:** Campaigns, Email, SMS, Forms, Landing Pages
**Analytics:** Dashboards, Reports, Funnel analysis
**Integrations:** Telephony, Email, WhatsApp, Payment gateways, Webhooks
**Platform:** Custom fields, custom modules (limited), API
**Vertical Solutions:** Edu, FSI, Real Estate, Healthcare

## 3. STRENGTHS (Top 10)

1. **Fast lead capture** — built for high-volume inbound (forms, chat, call, SMS)
2. **Excellent mobile app** — field sales focus, GPS visit logging, offline sync
3. **WhatsApp integration** — native WhatsApp Business API for sales communication
4. **Lead scoring + routing** — strong automated lead distribution rules
5. **Call/SMS integration** — native telephony in India-focused markets
6. **Affordable** — lower price point than Salesforce/HubSpot
7. **Good for high-velocity sales** — BFSI, EdTech with high lead volumes
8. **Vertical-specific solutions** — pre-built for Education, FSI, Real Estate
9. **Quick deployment** — simpler than Salesforce, faster to go live
10. **Good for team collaboration** — shared inbox, notes, activity feed

## 4. WEAKNESSES — OUR OPPORTUNITIES (Top 15)

1. **Limited enterprise features** — no sandboxes, no complex RBAC, no audit logs
2. **Account management is weak** — basic, no hierarchy or org structures
3. **No advanced forecasting** — pipeline is basic, no territory or quota management
4. **Limited customization** — custom objects are limited, no dynamic object builder
5. **UI/UX is outdated** — feels like 2010s design, not modern
6. **Performance issues** — slows down with large datasets
7. **No true offline** — mobile has limited offline, desktop needs connectivity
8. **Limited integrations** — smaller marketplace, less native connectors
9. **No true CPQ** — quotes are basic, no product catalog, no bundles
10. **Reporting is basic** — no custom dashboards in lower tiers
11. **No native CS/support** — no ticketing, SLA, knowledge base built-in
12. **Limited marketing automation** — basic email/SMS, no sophisticated journeys
13. **No AI features** — no lead scoring AI, no predictive, no conversation intelligence
14. **Limited API** — REST only, rate limits, no GraphQL
15. **Global presence is limited** — strong in India/APAC, weak in US/EU market

## 6. OUR OPPORTUNITIES AGAINST LEADSQUARED

1. **Modern UX** — LeadSquared feels dated. Clean, fast, delightful interface
2. **Built-in enterprise depth** — sandboxes, RBAC, audit, forecasting
3. **Dynamic Object Builder** — unlimited customization vs LeadSquared's rigid model
4. **True offline with CRDTs** — LeadSquared's offline is limited
5. **AI-native** — LeadSquared has no AI. We ship MCP/Ollama from day one
6. **Global from day one** — not India/APAC limited
7. **Better mobile + desktop parity** — full offline on both
8. **Integrated CS/Support** — not just sales CRM
