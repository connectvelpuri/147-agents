**Service Cloud:** Cases, Entitlements, Milestones, Omni-Channel, Knowledge Base, Service Console, Field Service, SLA Management

**Marketing Cloud:** Email Studio, Mobile Studio, Social Studio, Journey Builder, Datorama, Personalization

**Platform:** Custom Objects/Fields/Layouts, Apex, Visualforce, LWC, Flow, Process Builder, Approval Processes, REST/SOAP APIs, AppExchange

**Einstein AI:** Prediction Builder, Recommendation Builder, Bots, Language, Vision, Discovery, Analytics (Tableau CRM)

**Other:** Financial Services Cloud, Health Cloud, Revenue Cloud (CPQ), Experience Cloud, Slack

## 4. WEAKNESSES — OUR OPPORTUNITIES (Top 20)

1. **Expensive** — $150-300+/user/month + add-ons. Enterprise = $50K-500K/year
2. **Terrible UX** — cluttered, overwhelming, 10+ clicks for common tasks
3. **Slow performance** — page loads 3-10 seconds. Reports time out on large data
4. **No true offline** — limited and unreliable
5. **Poor data quality** — duplicates rampant despite Duplicate Rules
6. **Migration impossible** — proprietary data model, Apex lock-in
7. **Complex admin** — 6-12 month learning curve
8. **Force.com lock-in** — Apex/SOQL proprietary. Devs expensive
9. **Upgrades break code** — seasonal releases break customizations
10. **Poor support** — slow case resolution. Enterprise support costs extra
11. **Governor limits** — API calls, query rows, storage capped by edition
12. **No true single-tenant** — Hyperforce still shares infrastructure
13. **Limited native marketing** — Pardot/MC are separate, complex
14. **Mobile is an afterthought** — functional, not delightful
15. **Einstein AI expensive** — $50-150+/user/month add-on
16. **Reporting slow** — 30+ second runtimes on large datasets
17. **No built-in CS** — Health Score requires custom objects
18. **Partner management weak** — PRM separate, poorly integrated
19. **API limits strict** — 40K-500K calls/day by edition
20. **No modern AI agents** — Einstein Copilot early, expensive

## 6. OUR OPPORTUNITIES AGAINST SALESFORCE

1. Zero-latency local-first — Salesforce's biggest pain is speed
2. $0 core pricing — their #1 complaint
3. Privacy-first single-tenant — Enterprises WANT this
4. Modern UX — clean, fast, delightful
5. Easy migration path — import from Salesforce/HubSpot/spreadsheets
6. Dynamic Object Builder native — not an add-on
7. MCP-native AI — BYO-LLM, no Einstein lock-in
8. Integrated CS/Support — not separate clouds
9. No governor limits — you own the hardware
10. Simple administration — 1 hour to learn, not 6 months
